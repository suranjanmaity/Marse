About Us Winchester High Street's popular market food stall, Marse has now made it to its own takeaway and delivery shop in the heart of Winchester city. Marse offer hand made popular Nepalese street food made from all the goodness of ancient grains of amaranth, buckwheat, millet, spelt, rye, red rice, nigella and nine types of beans. Ancient grains are nutritious, delicious and sustainable. Follow us on instagram @marse.thenewdelicious
